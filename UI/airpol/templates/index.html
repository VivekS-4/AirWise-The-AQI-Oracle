Health Care & Precaution


For all age groups: Carry umbrellas, wear waterproof clothing, and non-slip footwear to prevent slips and falls.

For the elderly: Avoid getting soaked in the rain to prevent a sudden drop in body temperature. Keep warm and dry.

For those with health issues: Those prone to allergies should avoid damp areas, while individuals with joint pain might find warmth or using supportive footwear helpful to avoid slips.
